Guidance

Please give an indication of the following:

  • Details of any physical limitations.
  • Whether any aids are required and what aids are used.
  • Whether incontinence pads are used
  • Ensure all aspects of safety and supervision have been considered
  • Brief description of key restriction e.g. needs assistance due to back and knee pain or needs to lean on the window ledge to get on and off the toilet
  • Critical analysis of evidence
  • Review of inconsistencies and consider the usual features/progress/nature of the condition e.g. even though reports requiring assistance, had good movement of both upper limbs and good grip likely to support the reliable use of aids
  • Consider reliability and acceptable standard
  • Summarise and refer to the evidence gathered.
