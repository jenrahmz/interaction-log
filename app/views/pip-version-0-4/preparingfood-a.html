Guidance

Please give an indication of the following:

  • Whether specific aids are currently in use in relation to this activity
  • Whether they have ability to use an aid
  • Whether they use or are able to use a microwave.
  • Whether they can stand for any length of time.
  • If prompting is reported as taking place, clarify who does the prompting, how, and why, and what would happen if they were not there.
  • Ask about the whole process and whether they can plan and prepare a whole meal i.e. the end to end process.
  • Ensure all aspects of safety and supervision have been considered when preparing or cooking a meal are covered
  • Brief description of key restriction e.g. needs assistance due to fatigue and poor grip).
  • Brief description of key restriction.
  • Critical analysis of evidence e.g showed a restriction standing which is consistent with FE.
  • Review of inconsistencies and consider the usual features/progress/nature of the condition.
  • Consider safety.
  • Explain “sufficient” e.g. where assistance is claimed but HP chooses aids – this could be only mild grip noted in MSO for example which would be sufficient for the use of aids.
