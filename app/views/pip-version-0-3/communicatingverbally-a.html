Guidance

Please give an indication of the following:

  • Whether they wear hearing aids, and how many they
  • Whether they wear them all the time.
  • How they manage to hear and/or speak.
  • Give example(s) of how their hearing affects them day to day.
  • If there are speech restrictions or cognitive/intellectual restrictions – how these impact on their communication needs.
  • Whether any support is required including what format that ‘support’ takes e.g. BSL, Text to Speak etc.
  • Brief description of key restriction e.g. states uses BSL for all verbal communication due to profound deafness
  • Critical analysis of evidence e.g. showed was reliant on BSL throughout the assessment
  • Review of inconsistencies and consider the usual features/progress/nature of the condition
  • Summarise and refer to the evidence gathered.
